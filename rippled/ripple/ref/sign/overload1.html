sign (1 of 2 overloads)

Generate a signature for a message. 

Buffer
sign(
    PublicKey const& pk,
    SecretKey const& sk,
    Slice const& message);

With secp256k1 signatures, the data is first hashed with SHA512-Half, and the resulting digest is signed.
