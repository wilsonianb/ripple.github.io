generateSeed

Generate a seed deterministically. 

Seed
generateSeed(
    std::string const& passPhrase);

The algorithm is specific to Ripple: The seed is calculated as the first 128 bits of the SHA512-Half of the string text excluding any terminating null.

Remarks

This will not attempt to determine the format of the string (e.g. hex or base58).
